Our Story

Ann Arbor:
The Begining

When we first opened ZZ’s Produce in Ann Arbor we saw a great need within our community. With such a rich and diverse group of international neighbors, there was absent of a grocery store that catered to their native cuisines. Yes, there were a few locations around selling international items but the selection was small and/or the price was higher than one could afford.

That’s why; we opened in 2001, to give a store that not only gave the international community food from there respected countries but to also introduce the local community to these exotic and lively flavors of the world. As well as providing it at great low price.

The environment within ZZ’s is a place where one can visit and get a feel of an international street market. Where you can turn a corner and discover a new item each visit. Where one can learn of different cultures whether it be from a fellow customer eager to share a family recipe from back home or get a lesson from ZZ himself about an item’s background and how to cook with that ingredient.

We are forever grateful to the community for growing with us and helping us explore the variety of flavors that this world has to offer. Thank you for supporting our local street market store with a world of experiences.

Lansing

After a few years of building a great relationship with the community we were recieving visitors from further distances. Some customers reportedly driving over an hour just to see our selection. In November 2010, after much research and thought, we chose to open a second market north of Ann Arbor to accomodate the customers of Lansing and the surrounding area to provide our mission to other communities of rich and diverse ethnic backgrounds. Although possessing similar international flavors of the world, Lansing also included a palette of different cultural apetites. This gave us the opportunity to expand our resources and knowledge of international cuisine. Set out to become a market of the people by expanding our produce and grocery selection. We have also began to introduce various household items and personal care items.
